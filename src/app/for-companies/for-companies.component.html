Recruitment - International Nursing Recruitment

What advantages do foreign skilled workers offer?

The employment of international specialists enhances the global image, expands the network and develops international relationships.

The business model is of course not new in essence - neither the international recruitment of nursing staff, nor the permanent placement itself. What should be new, however, is the way of implementation and the quality path that we want to take for both customers and applicants.

Under the heading "Working, learning and living in Germany" we place the applicant or candidate at the center of our efforts. With this conceptual approach, we aim to invite motivated, medically and nursingly trained specialists from abroad within the framework of the freedom of movement for workers, but also from third countries to Germany, to put them into qualified service here in cooperation with customers, to support them in obtaining their professional recognition to support all levels and to develop further at the highest possible professional level. These specialists should be permanently bound to our customers and our healthcare system through our distinct welcoming culture.

The search for qualified specialists takes time and ties up human resources.

Our task is not only to bring you together with the right candidates, but above all to prepare and accompany them for the process of integration into the new environment. This ensures a higher success rate and ensures the sustainability of your staffing.

Our service process for you

We take care of the pre-selection of candidates who have a qualification that makes professional recognition possible.

All applicants have passed our screening. Our aim is to meet the needs of our customers with transparency, empathy and professionalism and to accompany the specialists on their way to a new life.

We organize the interview (by phone/Skype and in person) as well as the transfer of the candidates to you.

The biggest challenge is the language requirements. We have our own language coaches who specifically prepare our candidates for a B1 or B2 level (technical language).

As standard, we take care of all written and administrative matters from the preparation of the CV, the submission of applications, the (certified) translation of the documents for the recognition of degrees etc.

We personally advise each candidate throughout the process and report on it. If desired, we carry out intercultural training for integration in Germany or special technical language training.

HR Consulting - We increase your attractiveness as an employer and rationalize your duty scheduling!

We work closely together in consulting projects:

The Netherlands are 20 years ahead of Germany when it comes to making working hours more flexible in the healthcare sector. We - the consulting firm Noteboom & Van der Meulen and Eggert Kleffmann & Partner - use this advantage for you and thus accelerate your learning curve.

Employer-oriented flexibility dominates in Germany. Flexibility exists primarily as a control tool for corporate management. A conservative attitude often prevails in the organization of working hours in the care and health sector.

In an international comparison, the Netherlands has a high degree of employee-oriented working time flexibility. That means: in the Netherlands, the flexibilization of working hours primarily serves the interests of the employees.

In view of the inevitable worsening of the shortage of skilled workers, it is advisable for HR managers and nursing directors in Germany to look at developments in the Netherlands and to be open to new ideas and approaches for systemic flexibility and absence management.

We would be happy to tell you more about the flexibility concept in a personal conversation and how Noteboom & Van der Meulen has already introduced it to German hospitals and companies that care for the elderly!

In addition, we want to make our knowledge, our skills and our network available to everyone involved in order to bring about improvements in the system, in the organization and in intercultural cooperation.
