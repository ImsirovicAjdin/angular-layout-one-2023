Our service process for you

We take care of the pre-selection of candidates who have a qualification that makes professional recognition possible.

All applicants have passed our screening. Our aim is to meet the needs of our customers with transparency, empathy and professionalism and to accompany the specialists on their way to a new life.

We organize the interview (by phone/Skype and in person) as well as the transfer of the candidates to you.

The biggest challenge is the language requirements. We have our own language coaches who specifically prepare our candidates for a B1 or B2 level (technical language).

As standard, we take care of all written and administrative matters from the preparation of the CV, the submission of applications, the (certified) translation of the documents for the recognition of degrees etc.

We personally advise each candidate throughout the process and report on it. If desired, we carry out intercultural training for integration in Germany or special technical language training.
